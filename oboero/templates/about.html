About OBOERO

OBOERO 覚えろ
[imperative form] to remember or memorise.
When used wisely with an authoritative voice, it may come across as "Why don't you !@#$ remember stuff?"

A fun-packed Japanese language quiz where verb conjugation champs can pit their skils over their favourite ramen.
** Built with Flask (an awesome Python microframework!), HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery **
